About

I am a Business Analyst with experience in SQL, Excel, Python and other Business Intelligence tools like PowerBI and Tableau.

My experience as an analyst is mainly in the retail sector, where I have worked with a leading online retailer and provided support in customer analysis and decisioning. I can convert ambiguous problem statements to specific outputs and insights that can support business decisions.

I finished second in a national corporate case competition (EXL Acumen) (see features posts), where we solved an e-commerce case involving clickstream data, which got me a job offer.

I have an interest in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and Programming. Some of my projects can be found on my GitHub
